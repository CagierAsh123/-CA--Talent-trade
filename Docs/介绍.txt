[h1]人才贸易[/h1]

Phinix 的 Pawn 交易附属 mod，弥补超凡智能之间无法三角贸易的遗憾。

[h2]功能[/h2]

交易界面在 Phinix 界面中打开，与会话、交易同级，包含三个板块：

[b]直接交易[/b] — 玩家对玩家，实时协商，双方各自添加小人和银子，确认后同时交换。
[b]公共市场[/b] — 摆地摊，上架小人标价出售，其他玩家浏览购买，空投舱送达。
[b]租借系统[/b] — 出租你的小人，设定日租金、押金和最大天数。到期自动归还，死亡从备份复活。

[h2]技术说明[/h2]

Mod 采用序列化/反序列化传输 Pawn 数据，内置 XML 过滤，自动适应不同玩家的不同 mod 环境。
缺失的 mod 对应的装备、植入体等会在反序列化时被安全跳过，不会导致崩溃。（大概）

[h2]已知问题[/h2]

接收小人时日志中会出现若干 hediff/need 相关的警告（如图4），这是 RimWorld 原版 Scribe 系统的时序问题，不影响使用。
（泰南估计没想过有人会序列化反序列化pawn发给别人）

[h1] Talent Trade [/h1]

(Phinix’s Pawn Trade Add-on Mod)

This mod addresses the inability to conduct triangular trade between superintelligent entities (players).
This add-on for Phinix mod adds a comprehensive pawn (character) trading system.

[h2] Features [/h2]

The trade interface opens within the Phinix interface, at the same level as Chat and Trade, and includes three sections:

[b] Direct Trade [/b]
Player‑to‑player real‑time negotiation. Both sides can add pawns and silver; the exchange occurs simultaneously upon confirmation.

[b] Public Market [/b]
A bulletin‑board‑style market. List your pawns for sale, other players can browse and purchase; buyers receive the pawn via drop‑pod.

[b] Rental System [/b]
Lease your pawns: set daily rent, deposit, and maximum rental days. Pawns return automatically when the term expires; if they die, a backup copy is resurrected.

[h2] Technical Notes [/h2]

The mod uses serialization/deserialization to transmit pawn data. Built‑in XML filtering automatically adapts to different players’ mod setups.
Equipment, implants, etc., belonging to missing mods are safely skipped during deserialization—no crashes.（Maybe）

[h2] Known Issues [/h2]

Several hediff/need‑related warnings may appear in the log when receiving a pawn (see image 4). This is caused by a timing quirk of RimWorld’s vanilla Scribe system and does not affect functionality.
(Tynan probably never imagined anyone would serialize/deserialize pawns and send them to another player.)
